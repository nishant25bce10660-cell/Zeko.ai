About

The people building
AI for people

We're a small, focused team of researchers, designers, and engineers who believe AI's greatest potential lies in making humans more capable.

Our Mission

To create AI that
strengthens human connection

Founded in 2023, zeko.ai was born from a simple observation: the most transformative technology is the kind that disappears into the background, quietly making everything around it better.

We named ourselves after the concept of "善行" (zenko) — good deeds — because we believe technology should be an act of service.
